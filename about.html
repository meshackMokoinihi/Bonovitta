Welcome

Bonovitta Business Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a company that offers support to start-ups and small businesses. We assist our clients with compiling Business Documents, Sales, Marketing and Start-Up Support.

The need for this business came from realising that our country is filled with great entrepreneurs with valuable ideas but one thing that stops some from flourishing is lack of certain skills and information, we plan to bridge the gap. The company’s aim is to provide a helping hand by offering effective solutions to businesses in different sectors.
